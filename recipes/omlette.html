Odin Recipes

Description

A light, fluffy, and savory egg dish cooked in minutes — perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1–2 tbsp milk or water (optional, for fluffiness)
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • 1–2 tsp oil or butter

Steps

  1. Whisk: Beat eggs with salt, pepper, and milk/water.
  2. Prep Pan: Heat oil/butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Cook: Pour egg mixture. Tilt pan to spread evenly.
  4. Add Fillings: When semi-set, add fillings on one side.
  5. Fold: Cook until fully set. Fold and serve hot.
